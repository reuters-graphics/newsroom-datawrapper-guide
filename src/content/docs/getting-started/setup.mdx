Follow these steps to get your account setup and start charting with Datawrapper.

<Steps>
1. Email [all.graphics@tr.com](mailto:all.graphics@thomsonreuters.com) and ask the graphics team for a new Datawrapper account. 
    <Aside>If you're requesting access for someone else, be sure to include their TR email address with your request.</Aside>
2. You (or the persom you're requesting an account for) will receive an email confirming your new account from Datawrapper.
3. Go to [https://app.datawrapper.de/signin](https://app.datawrapper.de/signin) and login using your newly created Datawrapper account. 
4. Add yourself to the [Datawrapper Charts](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3A489aacae4e19400d8cf0da402c021076%40thread.skype/Datawrapper%20Charts?groupId=c0949280-94a2-469a-a719-12397824db0a&tenantId=62ccb864-6a1a-4b5d-8e1c-397dec1a8258) and [Datawrapper Helpline](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3A2cfe992b82e14e599bd7ad7eca59c324%40thread.skype/%E2%98%8E%EF%B8%8F%20Datawrapper%20helpline?groupId=c0949280-94a2-469a-a719-12397824db0a&tenantId=62ccb864-6a1a-4b5d-8e1c-397dec1a8258) channels in Teams.

    <Aside>You'll need to be a member of the [Reuters Graphics group](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/team/19%3Ad81f157f61c546ebb34e4392bff9554f%40thread.skype/conversations?groupId=c0949280-94a2-469a-a719-12397824db0a&tenantId=62ccb864-6a1a-4b5d-8e1c-397dec1a8258) in Teams to join these channels. If you're not a member, you can request to be added in Teams.</Aside>
</Steps>
